# CarFINDa

AI-powered car-finding web app that cuts through the chaos of used car shopping by aggregating listings, scoring each one against your specific needs and budget, and giving you a real-time AI advisor that explains every score, flags bad deals, warns about scams, and helps you negotiate — all in one place. It's built for first-time buyers who are overwhelmed by dealership tactics and scattered platforms, and who just want a straight answer: *is this car right for me?*
